Surfing is a must-do in Hawaii!

Our instructors are here to make the surfing experience accessible to everyone.

We offer beginner, intermediate, and advanced lessons.

Whether it's your first time in the ocean or you just want to brush up on your technique, we offer personalized lessons to benefit you as a surfer.

Our instructors can guarantee a fun, safe, and professional experience in the ocean.

All equipment and transportation is provided for your surf lesson in the Waikiki, Ala Moana, or Diamond head area.

Comfort. Safety.

Our lessons are incredibly safe and inclusive. We have extensive experience with small children, differently abled, and first time surfers.

Our instructors will go the extra mile to ensure your comfort in the ocean.

We only offer private lessons with a maximum of 4 surfers per instructor to make sure everyone gets the attention they need and deserve.

Go alone, as a couple, or with the family. No strangers or groups will be added to your lesson.

Our instructor will take you to a secluded location to surf away from the crowds of Waikiki.
